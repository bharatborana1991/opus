OpusLite

Source

1) Load a video

All processing stays in your browser.

2) Analyze

Heuristics: scene cuts + silence/energy peaks. No server, no upload.

3) Suggested clips

Tweak start/end, then export.

4) Export

Uses ffmpeg.wasm in your browser. First load may take a bit and will be cached for offline use by the app.

Notes

  • This is an independent, open-source demo inspired by shorts workflows. It’s not affiliated with Opus.pro.
  • Exact parity with commercial tools would require large ML models. This MVP focuses on client-only basics: scene/silence detection, clip suggestions, and export.
  • Optional captions & models can be added later. See README.
